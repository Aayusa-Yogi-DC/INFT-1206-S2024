1. Cooking Recipes to try this summer

I. Ingredients and Layers: An ice cream cake does not involve traditional cake layers. Instead, it is made of layers of ice cream and other goodies such as Oreo crunchies, Homemade chocolate fudge and Whipped cream.

II. Assembly steps:

  • Layer the ice cream: Line a cake pan with parchment paper. Add a layer of one ice cream flavor, followed by the Oreo crunchies, then another ice cream flavor.
  • Add the fudge: Spread homemade chocolate fudge over the top ice cream layer.
  • Freeze: Freeze the cake until firm.
  • Frost with whipped cream: Frost the cake with homemade whipped cream.
  • Decorate: Finish with rainbow confetti sprinkles.

2. Top Places to hike in this summer

I. Oshawa Creek Trail

  • This easy 8.5-kilometer trail winds its way along Oshawa Creek, offering a peaceful escape from the city.
  • The trail is perfect for a leisurely hike or bike ride, and there are plenty of spots to stop and enjoy the scenery.

II. Lakefront West Park Waterfront Trail:

  • This easy 2.5-kilometer trail offers stunning views of Lake Ontario.
  • The trail is perfect for a short walk or jog, and there are plenty of benches along the way to relax and enjoy the view.

3. Refreshing travel destinations

I. Niagara Falls:

  • Witness the power and majesty of Niagara Falls, one of the world's most incredible natural wonders.
  • Take a boat tour to get up close to the falls, visit Journey Behind the Falls for a unique perspective, or stroll along the Niagara Parkway for breathtaking views.

II. Georgian Bay

  • Boasting over 30,000 islands, Georgian Bay is a paddler's paradise.
  • Explore the sheltered bays and inlets of the bay by kayak or canoe, camp on a secluded island, or go scuba diving in the shipwrecks that litter the bottom of the bay.
