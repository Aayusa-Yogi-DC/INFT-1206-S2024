A short introduction video for Markhu by Nepal Tourism Board.

Markhu is a beautiful village that lies around the famous man-made Kulekhani Dam in Nepal. This place lies in the Indrasarowar Rural Municipality of Makwanpur District in the Bagmati Province of Nepal. Moreover, this place is one of the nearest places from Kathmandu to escape from Kathmandu Valley with your friends and family.
